About Us

Godai Combat Shotokan Karate-Do Association

GCSKA is a registered body under Government of India. GCSKA has been undertaking the responsibility of training people for many years since its establishment. Over the time it has produced many good sports person who have gained expertise and are taking forward our vision not only in India but also in some neighbouring countries like Nepal. GCSKA team has won many accolades in various national level tournaments being conducted by various recognised bodies. GCSKA founder Rensei Uttam Bhat hails from Nepal and is a 7th Dan Black belt in Shotokan style. He brings to us more than 25 years of rich experience in this form of art. He is also a permanent instructor in many schools as also gives private training to many professionals in the bollywood industry.

Vision

To sow the seeds of determination and confidence in the minds of the people, make them physically and mentally tough to take on the challenges in life, develop the sportsmanship spirit and thereby help them live a happy and prosperous life.

Foreword

I would like to appreciate and express my thankfulness to all members of the association for the support they have provided to take the initiative of spreading GCSKA's vision to the mankind. This association is the step towards encouraging the person to learn Karate techniques and take the challenges and opportunities which the life throws at us with much confidence and positive attitude.
